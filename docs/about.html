Our Product

MysticAlign is an interactive experience that blends tarot readings, astrology horoscopes, and MBTI personality types into a social, reflective daily ritual. The platform creates a personalized feed of daily insights and allows users to compare readings and spiritual vibes with their friends.

Team Sweet 16: Our Values
